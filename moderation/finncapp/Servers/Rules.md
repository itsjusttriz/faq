## Subscriber Server Rules

⚠️**These servers are ONLY available to Subscribers & specially permitted community members!**

- Sharing the server details with non-permitted members will result in a ban from all servers **and** the stream.
- Anyone known to be performing/displaying/creating inappropriate structures/images/movements will be dealt with accordingly.
- For servers that provide and require them, overflow protection is a **MUST**.
- Failure to comply with these rules alongside any further ruling made by staff will result in a removal, be it access to the servers, access to the Discord or Twitch, or all of the above and further (depending on level of offense).
- This ruleset is subject to change at any given time. Be prepared. Be honorable. **BE GOOD!**
- Streaming from this server, **is** allowed. Just be respectful to others as they should, to you.
- Chance Cubes are permitted on a case-by-case basis. If you're not permitted, yet have the ability to trigger a cube, do **not** abuse this. Report it, instead.

## Community Server Rules

⚠️**These servers are ONLY available to active & permitted members of the community!**

- Sharing the server details with non-permitted members will result in a ban from all servers **and** the stream.
- Anyone known to be performing/displaying/creating inappropriate structures/images/movements will be dealt with accordingly.
- For servers that provide and require them, overflow protection is a **MUST**.
- While it is not required, it is highly encouraged and appreciated for community members to contribute a little something to help out with server costs. While your fun and entertainment may be free, the servers are not.
- Having subscriber status does not provide immediate access to Community Servers. You must be a well-known and an approvably interactive member of this community.
- Failure to comply with these rules alongside any further ruling made by staff will result in a removal, be it the Community Server Discord role, access to the servers, access to the Discord or Twitch, or all of the above and further (depending on level of offense).
- This ruleset is subject to change at any given time. Be prepared. Be honorable. **BE GOOD!**
